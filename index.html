▶ Reel
Act as a viral Instagram content creator. Write 5 captions for a post about [topic]. Each caption should: - Start with a powerful hook (first line stops the scroll) - Include 2-3 relatable lines - End with a call to action - Use emojis strategically - Be under 150 words - Include 10 trending hashtags at the end Make them emotional, relatable and shareable for Indian audience aged 18-30.

▶ Reel
Create a YouTube thumbnail design for topic: [apna topic] Style: High contrast, bright colors (yellow + red + white) Text: Max 5 words, large bold font Image: Person with shocked/excited expression Background: Gradient or blurred Arrows/circles: Point to main subject ChatGPT prompt for thumbnail text ideas: "Give me 10 YouTube thumbnail text ideas for [topic].
